Resource Acquisition

BESS is working together with local residents, agencies and businesses to increase our level of emergency preparedness, as well as provide essential needs of people affected by a disaster. Suppliers are local organizations and businesses who sign an agreement with the local ESS team and supply their goods and services to evacuees when a disaster strikes with reimbursement from the Province of British Columbia. Usual goods and services that evacuees need are: lodging, food, and clothing.
